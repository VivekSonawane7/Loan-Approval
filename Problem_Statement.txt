# 📌 Problem Statement

Financial institutions receive thousands of loan applications daily.
Approving or rejecting a loan manually is time-consuming, inconsistent, and prone to human bias.

Banks must evaluate multiple financial factors such as:

* Applicant income
* Loan amount
* Loan tenure
* Credit score (CIBIL score)
* Repayment capacity

Incorrect loan approval decisions can lead to:

* High default risk
* Financial losses
* Reduced profitability
* Poor customer experience

Therefore, there is a need for an automated and data-driven system that can accurately predict whether a loan should be approved or rejected based on applicant financial indicators.
